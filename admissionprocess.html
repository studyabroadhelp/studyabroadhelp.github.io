Step-by-Step Admission Process

Step 1: Eligibility Check

Ensure you have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, meet the minimum percentage, and qualified NEET.

Step 2: Document Preparation

Prepare mark sheets, passport, NEET certificate, medical fitness, and photos as required by the university.

Step 3: Admission Application

Fill out the online application form of the chosen university or through our guidance platform.

Step 4: Admission Letter & Visa

After verification, receive the Admission Letter (LOA) and apply for a student visa to Kyrgyzstan.

Step 5: Travel & Orientation

Arrive in Kyrgyzstan with guidance on airport pickup, hostel, and university orientation.

Step 6: Start MBBS Classes

Begin your MBBS journey in English medium, with hospital exposure and international student support.
