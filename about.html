Why Study MBBS in Kyrgyzstan?

  • Affordable tuition fees compared to India
  • No donation or capitation fees
  • English medium of instruction
  • WHO and NMC approved universities
  • Safe environment for international students
  • Good patient exposure

Recognition & Accreditation

  • Recognized by National Medical Commission (NMC), India
  • Listed in World Directory of Medical Schools (WDOMS)
  • Approved by World Health Organization (WHO)
  • Eligible for FMGE / NEXT examination

Graduates are eligible to practice in India after qualifying the required screening examination.

About Osh International Medical University

Osh International Medical University is located in Osh, Kyrgyzstan. It provides quality medical education for international students with affordable fees and a safe environment.
