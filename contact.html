Apply for MBBS in Kyrgyzstan

This website is created to provide genuine guidance for students who want to study MBBS in Kyrgyzstan.

Required Documents

  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet
  • NEET Scorecard
  • Passport (first & last page)
  • Passport size photographs

Application Process

  1. Document verification
  2. University offer letter
  3. Admission confirmation
  4. Visa processing
  5. Departure to Kyrgyzstan

Note: This website does not claim to be an agent or consultancy. Students are advised to verify all information independently.
