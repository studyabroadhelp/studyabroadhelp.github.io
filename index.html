About Studying MBBS in Kyrgyzstan

Kyrgyzstan has become one of the most trusted destinations for Indian students and parents who are looking for affordable, safe, and quality medical education abroad. Universities in Kyrgyzstan follow international medical standards and focus strongly on practical clinical training.

Parents feel confident because of low living costs, Indian food availability, safe hostels, and globally accepted medical degrees.

Fees Structure

MBBS in Kyrgyzstan is much more affordable compared to private medical colleges in India. The average tuition fee ranges between ₹2.5 to ₹4 lakhs per year.
